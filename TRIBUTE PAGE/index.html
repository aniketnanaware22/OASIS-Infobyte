Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an eminent Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a vital role in India’s civilian space program and military missile development.

Major Achievements

  • Known as the Missile Man of India
  • Key role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
  • Inspired millions through his vision for youth

Famous Quote

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
